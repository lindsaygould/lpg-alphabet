About this work

This selection is drawn from over 3,000 alphabet photographs shot on Fuji Velvia 35mm slide film by artist and photographer Laura Paresky Gould in the 1990s as part of her Graphic Axioms series. Letter "E" photographs from the series were animated by the artist into an award-winning identity campaign for E! Entertainment Television, widely seen during the O.J. trial. The Graphic Axioms series was exhibited in solo shows at G. Ray Hawkins Gallery and Robert Klein Gallery and is held in museums and collections worldwide.

About the artist

Laura Paresky Gould is a RISD-trained visual artist and motion designer whose practice spans photography, animation, and immersive installations. She began her career creating award-winning motion graphics and brand identities for Nike, NBC, and CBS, and later served as Design Director at Ocean Drive magazine. Her photography has appeared in The New York Times, Vogue, and Condé Nast Traveller, and is included in museum, public, and private collections.
