Each letter is composed from a photograph by Laura Paresky Gould. Miami Color Theory is her ongoing study of color, light, and composition in the everyday — Art Deco facades, lifeguard stations, reflections, shadows, and Miami's pastel palette. A Duke graduate based in Miami, Paresky Gould is the author of Seeing Differently (TRA Publishing).
